About Marc Hadley

Marc Hadley is an undergraduate student at Illinois State University. He majors in Creative Technologies: Game Design. Marc also works at TechZone Service Center as a hardware technician. He is a 4th yeah who wants to go into Creative Writing for games. This includes script writing, game direction, and plot development. He currently lives in Normal, IL. In his free time, Marc writes, plays video games, or works on other passion projects such as music creation, game development, or character design. He gets most of his insiration from Dungeons and Dragons and games such as Elden Ring, Kingdom Hearts, and God of War.
